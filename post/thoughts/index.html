thoughts

12th June 2015

So, I am set to graduate from Stanford this weekend and I don’t feel happy or accomplished at all. Failed a drive exam today, so I’ve now failed a driving test on two continents :P. I’m going to get a lot of shit about that from my family.

Sometimes I wonder if its a good thing or bad that things that should make me happy don’t or not happy enough. There’s always something else that needs to be done, something better more meaningful, more valuable, more worthy, more satisfying. This is probably not very healthy. Luckily, I can suppress such thoughts most of the time.

I’m trying to figure out what Success means to me ? Right now I’m circling the drain of

  • Family and health
  • Good friends
  • Well it has to be money.
  • It’s doing what I want when I want.
    • how is that possible ? Answer: Money

More worryingly, I don’t know if I have life as figured out as I want it to be. I don’t exactly love CS, definitely don’t want to do it all my life. I’m not even an expert at any one sub-field.

I’ve been feeling uneasy all day, went and had Philz coffee, scoop ice-cream but wasn’t distracted. I guess the thing that makes me feel uneasy is that I don’t know the answer to the questions:

  • Is the startup I turned down going to become huge and I miss out on millions [See Google Docs for the list of reasons I wrote down]
  • Will I be successfull by my definition ? Will I make a lot of money ? Will that make me happy ?
  • Is that enough to exit the rat-race ? Do I have the courage to try exiting the rat-race ?
